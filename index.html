More About Me

I recently (August 2022) graduated with a master's degree in Engineering Design from The Pennsylvania State University. My interests revolve around the design and development of products, Additive Manufacturing (AM), and the push for an innovative & human-centered ecosystem.

Background

Born and raised in Lebanon, I grew up in Ain el Rihane, a small town in the "Mount Lebanon" district. I spent my entire school years at Collège des Frères Maristes Champville, which follows a strict French system, and where I learned and became fluent in English, French, and Arabic. I proceeded with my journey by attending the Lebanese American University - Byblos, also stationed in Lebanon, majoring in Mechanical Engineering.

Acknowledging the unstable economic, political, and social circumstances in Lebanon (with the lack of promising career opportunities), I boosted my GPA and transferred to The California State University-Chico to finish my B.S in Mechanical Engineering. I graduated in May 2021 with a cumulative GPA of 3.085 (3.6 during my Junior and Senior years). I also got the opportunity to pursue my academic journey by completing my master's degree in Engineering Design from the Pennsylvania State University. I have acquired experience in the various stages of product development, including but not limited to idea generation, 3D modeling, and customer experience. Furthermore, I expanded my skills with electronics (Arduino, RasPi, motor control) and programming (front and back-end).
